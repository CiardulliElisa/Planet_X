PLANET X

Planet X is the digital version of the homonymous board game. In Planet X, players take on the role of astronomers who use observations and logical deductions to search for this hypothetical planet. In each game, a randomly selected arrangement of objects and a location for Planet X are generated following predefined logic rules.
